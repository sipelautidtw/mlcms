## 11. CONTOH KONTEN "EMAS"

### Contoh 1 — Reference dari Blog Maritime Lentera
**Platform:** WordPress / Blog (maritimelentera.com)
**Topik:** Hydrodynamic Ship Design
**Konten (excerpt):**
```
Hydrodynamic ship design is at the core of the maritime industry's shift toward greater fuel efficiency and sustainability. As vessel performance, environmental compliance, and operational cost savings become top priorities, optimizing how ships interact with water is more important than ever.

At PT. Lentera Segara Indonesia, we combine modern engineering principles with real-world maritime experience to deliver vessel designs that excel in fuel efficiency and hydrodynamic performance.
```
**Mengapa ini "Maritime Lentera banget":**
- Hook membuka dengan industry trend yang relevan (sustainability, fuel efficiency)
- Statement company di paragraf kedua dengan posisi credible
- Tidak overselling — menjelaskan "what we do" tanpa klaim superlatif
- Bahasa otoritatif tanpa terlalu jargon-heavy

**Platform:** WordPress

```
Ship design workflow plays a vital role in the success of any maritime project. To create reliable, efficient vessels, every design phase must be well-structured—from initial concept to finalized blueprint.

To begin with, the process starts with a conceptual study. This phase defines the vessel's purpose, speed, capacity, and regulatory requirements. In this early stage, designers collaborate closely with stakeholders to align on goals and functionality.

Next, the team transitions into preliminary layout design...
```
**Mengapa ini bagus:**
- Struktur educational yang clear — pakai signpost ("To begin with", "Next", "Importantly")
- Penjelasan teknis tapi accessible — tidak mengasumsikan pembaca tahu segalanya
- Konsisten dengan positioning "edukatif & otoritatif"
